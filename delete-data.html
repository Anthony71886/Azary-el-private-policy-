Delete Data Request

You can request deletion of your personal data from Light of the Chosen at any time.

  1. Send an email to support@lightofthechosen.app from the same address associated with your account (if applicable).
  2. Include: your name, the email used in the app, and “Delete My Data” in the subject line.

We will confirm receipt and permanently remove your personal data from our systems within 30 days, unless a longer period is required by law. If we must retain certain data (e.g., legal/transaction records), we will restrict it to that purpose only.

Questions? Email us at support@lightofthechosen.app.
