Data Deletion

App: Azary’el Flame: Light of the Chosen
Effective Date: September 9, 2025

Overview

This page explains how you can delete data related to Azary’el Flame: Light of the Chosen (“the App”). The App does not create user accounts or collect personally identifiable information directly. The App displays ads using Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data to serve and measure ads.

What data exists

  • On your device: local app cache/configuration that you can clear.
  • Advertising-related: device advertising identifiers and ad interaction data handled by Google/AdMob.

How to delete data

  1. Uninstall the App
    Removing the App from your device deletes all locally stored app data.
  2. Clear app data (Android)
    Go to Settings → Apps → Azary’el Flame → Storage → Clear data to remove local cache/configuration.
  3. Reset or delete your Advertising ID (Android)
    Go to Settings → Privacy → Ads and choose Reset advertising ID or Delete advertising ID (wording may vary by Android version). This helps remove/refresh the identifier used for personalized ads.
  4. Opt out of personalized ads
    Visit Google Ads Settings to manage ad personalization.
